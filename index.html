Delivery service accessibility in USA

GIS for Design Practices Kitae & Julia

Package Delivery

There are many package delivery service providers across the United States, such as UPS, USPS, FedEx, and Amazon, and they are widely distributed. While delivery service providers like UPS are spread across most regions, Amazon is mostly located in large cities and along highways.

Grocery Delivery

Across the United States, there are various grocery brands, each offering delivery services within a radius of up to 15 miles. Target has 1,780 locations, Trader Joe's has 521, Walmart has 5,234, and Whole Foods has 517 locations. Walmart has the highest number of stores and the widest coverage area.

Meal Delivery

Meal delivery services are also growing continuously, with most deliveries made by bicycle or car. These services tend to operate only in cities, depending on the location of the delivery person and the restaurant. DoorDash serves the smallest areas, while UberEats operates in the largest regions.

Delivery Desert

Approximately 4 million households in the U.S. live in food deserts, where access to groceries is limited. Due to the lack of nearby grocery stores, they may rely on delivery services as an alternative to obtain food. However, many areas in the U.S. still lack grocery and meal delivery services, some of which are also classified as food deserts.
